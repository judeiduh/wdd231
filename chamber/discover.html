Discover Anuka

Welcome to Anuka, a vibrant city with a rich history and thriving business community. Founded in 1952, Anuka has grown from a small trading post to a bustling economic hub with a population of over 250,000 residents.

Demographics

  • Population: 253,742 (2023 estimate)
  • Median Age: 34.5 years
  • Household Income: $62,450 (median)
  • Education: 42% with bachelor's degree or higher
  • Major Industries: Technology, Manufacturing, Healthcare, Education

Local Attractions

Business Environment

Anuka offers a thriving business climate with:

  • Low corporate tax rates
  • Skilled workforce
  • Excellent transportation infrastructure
  • Business development grants
  • Incubator programs for startups

The Chamber of Commerce works closely with local government to create a welcoming environment for businesses of all sizes.
